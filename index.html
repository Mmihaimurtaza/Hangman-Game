Hangman

HOW TO PLAY

The Game
Hangman is a classic word game in which you must guess the secret word one letter at a time. Guess one letter at a time to reveal the secret word. Each incorrect guess adds another part to the hangman. You only get 6 incorrect guesses.

The Points
You can score up to 2 points for each correct word. If you get 5 correct answers in a row, you'll get 5 more extra points. Try to keep your streak going!

Hints
If you need help finding a word, click the Hint button . Each hint costs 1 point from the maximum points that you can win(2). You can use up to 2 hints per round.
